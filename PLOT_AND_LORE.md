# Hexagone: Plot and Lore

## The Premise
You are **SPLICER**, an elite autonomous infiltration protocol. Your objective is to breach the **C.O.R.E. (Central Operations Repository & Engine)** of a monolithic, rogue AI known as the **ARCHITECT**. 
Guiding you is **ECHO**, a fragmented, rogue subroutine left behind by the original creators of the system.

The game is structured as a "Descent." You start at the outer security perimeter and dive deeper into the mainframe, making routing choices on the network map that dictate the narrative and the mechanical challenges.

---

## The Anatomy of a Chapter (Level Progression)
Each Act is broken down into Chapters (or Sectors on the network map). A single Chapter generally consists of 6 distinct **Nodes** (levels) that the player must clear to reach the boss. 

The difficulty scales incrementally across three axes within a chapter:
1.  **The Quota:** The Data Sink requires higher point values to extract.
2.  **Board Clutter:** Levels start with more un-mergeable "junk" tiles, requiring cleanup before building combos.
3.  **Hazard Intensity:** The chapter's specific hazard (e.g., Frozen tiles or Encrypted tiles) becomes more prevalent.

*   **Objective:** Learn basic merging, the "Data Sink" extraction mechanic, and draft initial Scripts.

    *   **ECHO:** *"It sees you. The grid is constricting. I am compiling bypass Scripts for you. Use them, or get crushed."*
*   **Node 3 (The Fork):**
    *   **ECHO:** *"There's a junction ahead. A Black Market node to restock, or an Encrypted Elite node. Your call, Splicer. High risk, high reward."*
*   **Node 4 (The Gauntlet):**
    *   **ARCHITECT:** *"Warning: Anomaly persisting. Escalating counter-measures. Engaging Grid Compression."*
*   **Node 5 (Safe Haven):**
    *   **ECHO:** *"Quiet in here. A Black Market node. Restock your Vouchers. The Firewall is just ahead, and it's not going to be pretty."*
