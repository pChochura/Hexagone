# Hexagone: The Relic Tree (Meta-Progression)

## Overview
The Relic Tree is the permanent meta-progression system in Hexagone. Even if a player fails a run, they retain the **Data Fragments** they collected. These fragments are spent in the main menu to permanently upgrade SPLICER's base capabilities.

### Earning Data Fragments
*   **Node Completion:** Base amount awarded for clearing standard nodes.
*   **Elite Nodes & Bosses:** Massive payouts for clearing high-difficulty encounters.
*   **Side Quests/Missions:** Bonus fragments awarded for completing in-run challenges (e.g., "Clear a node in under 15 turns" or "Perform a x10 combo").
*   **Data Conversion:** If a player abandons a run or dies, their remaining in-run currency is converted into Data Fragments at a severe penalty (e.g., 10 to 1).

---

## The Relic Tree Structure
The tree is divided into three dependent branches. Players must unlock and upgrade preceding nodes to access deeper, more powerful abilities. 

**Upgrade Levels:** Every Relic node has multiple levels (e.g., Lvl 1 to Lvl 5). Upgrading a Relic exponentially increases its Data Fragment cost but scales its effects. Higher-tier Relics remain locked until the preceding Relic in the branch reaches a specific minimum level.

### Root Node (Tier 1)
*   **Initialization [Max Lvl: 1, Base Cost: 50 Fragments]:** Activates the Splicer Protocol. Permanently unlocks the appearance of "Black Market" shop nodes on the network map.
    *(Requires Initialization Lvl 1 to unlock Tier 2 branches)*

---

### Branch A: The Architect's Code (Economy & Drafting)
*Focuses on maximizing resource generation, improving Script drafts, and manipulating the Black Market.*

*   **Tier 2: Overclocked Mining [Max Lvl: 5]:** Permanently increases Data Fragment yield from all sources by +5% per level (Max +25%).
*   **Tier 3: Black Market VIP [Max Lvl: 3]:** *(Requires Overclocked Mining Lvl 3)* Prices at the Black Market for Vouchers and Scripts are reduced by -10% per level (Max -30%).
*   **Tier 4: Expanded Memory [Major - Max Lvl: 2]:** *(Requires VIP Lvl 3)* Lvl 1 grants a **4th Active Script Slot**. Lvl 2 grants a **5th Active Script Slot**, allowing for vastly more complex build synergies.
*   **Tier 5: The Genesis Draft [Ultimate - Max Lvl: 1]:** *(Requires Expanded Memory Lvl 2)* Script Draft screens now present 4 options instead of 3, and you are guaranteed to see at least one *Rare* or higher Script in every draft.

---

### Branch B: The Splicer's Edge (In-Run Mechanics)
*Focuses on raw gameplay buffs, starting loadouts, and scoring potential.*

*   **Tier 2: Cache Allocation [Max Lvl: 3]:** Start every single run with +1 Common Voucher per level (Move, Swap, Remove).
*   **Tier 3: Combo Momentum [Max Lvl: 4]:** *(Requires Cache Allocation Lvl 2)* Your Combo Multiplier starts at x2 (Lvl 1), x3 (Lvl 2), x4 (Lvl 3), or x5 (Lvl 4) at the beginning of every node.
*   **Tier 4: Tactical Loadout [Major - Max Lvl: 2]:** *(Requires Combo Momentum Lvl 3)* Lvl 1: Start runs with 1 Rare 'Swap' and 1 Mimic Voucher. Lvl 2: Add 1 Legendary 'Path Merge' Voucher.
*   **Tier 5: Ascension Protocol [Ultimate - Max Lvl: 1]:** *(Requires Tactical Loadout Lvl 2)* When you trigger the "Save Me" (Revive) prompt during a run, it now additionally executes a 50% board wipe—instantly deleting the lowest value half of your board to give you massive breathing room.

---

### Branch C: The Network Override (System Manipulation)
*Focuses on manipulating the map routing, softening Boss encounters, and avoiding bad RNG.*

*   **Tier 2: Route Mapping [Max Lvl: 3]:** Lvl 1 reveals Sector Bosses. Lvl 2 reveals Elite Node types. Lvl 3 reveals the exact location of all Black Markets in the Act.
*   **Tier 3: Node Override [Max Lvl: 3]:** *(Requires Route Mapping Lvl 2)* Grants 1 reroll per Act (Lvl 1), 2 rerolls (Lvl 2), or 3 rerolls (Lvl 3) to convert an Encrypted (Elite) Node into a Standard Node.
*   **Tier 4: Firewall Breach [Major - Max Lvl: 2]:** *(Requires Node Override Lvl 2)* Boss mechanics are permanently weakened by 15% (Lvl 1) or 30% (Lvl 2).
*   **Tier 5: Sandbox Privileges [Ultimate - Max Lvl: 1]:** *(Requires Firewall Breach Lvl 2)* Once per entire run, you may completely bypass any non-boss node of your choice. You instantly receive the completion rewards and move forward without risking a system crash.
